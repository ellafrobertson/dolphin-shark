Dolphin vs Shark Classifier

An AI-Powered Marine Life Classification Model

About the Business

The Dolphin vs Shark Classifier is a cutting-edge AI tool developed to assist marine conservationists, ecotourism businesses, and educators. Our goal is to leverage technology to improve understanding and protection of marine life.

Created by Ella Robertson, this project combines state-of-the-art machine learning techniques with a passion for marine conservation.

Mission Statement

Our mission is simple: To harness the power of AI to classify marine species, support conservation efforts, and educate people worldwide about the importance of protecting our oceans.

Key Features

  • Custom-trained AlexNet model with 85% validation accuracy.
  • Diverse dataset of dolphin and shark images.
  • Applications in research, conservation, and ecotourism.
  • Supports marine life education initiatives.

How It Works

Upload an image of a dolphin or shark, and our model will classify it with high accuracy. The AI model uses convolutional neural networks to extract features and make predictions.
